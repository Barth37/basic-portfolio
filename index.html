About Me

My name is Brian Arth and I am, more or less, a returning student. I spent my work career in bank for 12 yrs before taking on the mantly of "Mr. Mom". Not sure which of those was more difficult, but I can tell you which one smelled the worst. My children are all in school so my workload has dramatically dropped so I decided that it was time to return to the work force.


As exciting as banking sounds, it wasn't. I was in every area at some point or another; collections, customer service, risk, fraud, lending, etc. Though they were interesting at times, it wasn't anything to build a career on. So now that I have the time and resources, I decided that it was time to look into what I wanted to do and avoid the duldroms of "just any job".


Through much deliberation, I arrived at computers. I am fairly competent using a computer, but I had no real idea how they do what they do. I did a lot of reading and realized that coding was the answer. So here I am. Wish me luck!
