About Me

I was born and raised in a very very small town in PA. I went to school there from Kindergarten to grad school. Upon graduation, I got a job at a bank, MBNA. I worked there for several years before taking a job as a lon officer in Keybank's recreation lending division. That division did lending for boats, yachts, and RV's. Things I will never have so it was a nice glimpse into "how the other half live."


I got married in '06 and soon followed our first child. Once my wife's maternity leave ended, I decided that an early retirement was in order so I took the mantle of Mr. Mom. 10 years, I did this until my marriage sadly ended. Back to work I go, but this time, it will be in something fun and interesting. Coding it is!!
